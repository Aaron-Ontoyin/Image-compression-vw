About Me

I embarked on this project driven by my passion for the world of digital media and a strong desire to make a tangible impact. As an avid photographer and content creator, I often faced the challenge of managing large image files. This struggle inspired me to create a solution that would not only simplify my life but also benefit others facing similar issues. The idea for my image compression web app was born from these personal experiences and the vision of making image optimization accessible to all.

This project was developed as a personal endeavor and a demonstration of my skills. It is also a part of my portfolio as a student of Holberton School.
